# Product

## Register

product

## Users

The support-services department of شركة زمام القوة — roughly 10–15 people across administration, logistics, and procurement.

- **Managers / admins** work mostly on desktop: they assign and approve tasks, run procurement (quotations), and manage the fleet, assets, maintenance, and suppliers.
- **Members** are the day-to-day staff, often on a phone in the field: they see only the tasks assigned to them and act on them (update status, comment, attach a photo/invoice).
- Bilingual team, **Arabic-first** with full RTL; numbers are always Western (1234567890), never Eastern-Arabic.

The job to be done: run the department's daily operations — tasks, recurring duties, procurement, fleet, assets, and maintenance — from one shared system, with the right person seeing the right slice.

## Product Purpose

«إدارة الخدمات المساندة» is the department's single operations system. It replaces scattered spreadsheets and chat with one shared, real-time source of record for:

- **Tasks & approvals** (assignment, recurring duties, procurement quotations)
- **Assets** (a unified registry: individually-tracked items and bulk/quantity stock)
- **Fleet** (vehicles, fuel, documents)
- **Maintenance** (one centralized hub across fleet/equipment/facilities)
- **Suppliers** (registry + data-driven evaluation)

Success = the team manages a day's real work without leaving the app, on phone or desktop, with nothing lost between people.

## Brand Personality

Trustworthy & solid, calm & professional. It should read like a dependable **system of record**, not a flashy product: quiet confidence, precise, easy on the eyes for long sessions. Voice is plain and specific in clear Arabic business terminology; labels say what will happen. Earthy, grounded palette over bright/loud.

## Anti-references

- **Generic card-grid SaaS** — endless identical cards, the hero-metric template, the default AI dashboard look.
- **Cluttered government portals** — dense, disorganized, hard-to-scan screens.
- Also avoid: flashy gradients/marketing theatrics, decorative motion, anything that feels like a toy rather than a tool.

## Design Principles

1. **Action over analysis.** Surface the next action, not a wall of charts. Members see what to do; managers see what to approve. Mobile is task-first by design.
2. **One source of truth.** Every record (task, asset, maintenance log) lives in exactly one place and appears wherever it's relevant — no duplicates, no drift.
3. **Calm density.** Show enough to decide without clutter. Restraint over decoration; whitespace and hierarchy do the work.
4. **Arabic-first dignity.** Professional Arabic terminology, full RTL parity, Western numerals — the Arabic experience is the primary one, not a translation afterthought.
5. **Trust through precision.** Accurate states and honest feedback; destructive actions are confirmed; offline keeps working and syncs cleanly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target **WCAG AA** contrast on text (≥4.5:1 body, ≥3:1 large); never rely on light-gray-on-tint for body copy — readable for older users and in bright/outdoor conditions.
- Keep the **S/M/L text-size** control easily reachable.
- Honor **prefers-reduced-motion** (animations have reduced/instant alternatives).
- Full **RTL** correctness for Arabic; status meaning shouldn't depend on color alone where feasible (pair with label/icon).
